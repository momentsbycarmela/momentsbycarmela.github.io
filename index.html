ABOUT ME

~This website for me is a way to showcase my passion for photography and I would love for my work to be seen as a a celebration of nature and the human endeavour.
~I shoot both digital and on film but I always try and use film as I have a real appreciation for the way they turn out once you develop them.
~My style of shooting in a fast, fluid fashion to create situations that encourage and allow my subjects to have a freedom to express and enjoy themselves.
~When I shoot peoples' portraits they usually are of people who don't model professionally, so I always allow plenty of opportunity to have a conversation before and during the shoot.
~Much of my photography incorporates natural elements, so I always make sure that when I shoot I am not disturbing or causing damage to my surroundings.
~My individual approach to photography produces genuine, natural moments as they unfold. This keeps shoots spontaneous and full of energy, so that to the best of my ability I am able to produce an emotive final image.
